# Privacy Policy for Crescent Gate

**Last updated: December 10, 2025**

## 1. Introduction
Crescent Gate ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and discloses your information.

## 2. Data We Collect
- **Personal Information:** Name, Email, Phone Number, Wing, Flat Number.
- **Visitor Data:** Names, Photos (for verification), Phone numbers of visitors.
- **Device Information:** Device ID (for Push Notifications via OneSignal).

## 3. How We Use Data
- To verify identity for secure gate access.
- To send real-time notifications about visitors and alerts.
- To maintain a digital registry of society activities.

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:
- **Supabase:** For secure Cloud Database storage.
- **OneSignal:** For delivering push notifications.

## 5. Your Rights
You may request deletion of your account and data by contacting the Society Admin.

## 6. Contact Us
For privacy concerns, please contact: privacy@crescentgate.com
